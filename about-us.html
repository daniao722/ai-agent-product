Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of digital services including web strategy and planning, UI/UX design, web development, digital marketing, brand identity design, and ongoing support and maintenance.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while complex web applications or full brand overhauls may require 8-12 weeks or more.

What is your pricing model?

We work with both fixed-price projects and hourly rates, depending on your needs. After a discovery phase, we provide a detailed proposal outlining scope, deliverables, and transparent pricing.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes! We offer flexible ongoing support packages that include maintenance, updates, security monitoring, and performance optimization to keep your digital presence running smoothly.
